Project Brief

Description

Elliot Lake College is a new college that is in northern Ontario, and it is in an area that is surrounded by lakes, rivers, and forests. Also, Elliot Lake College will focus on digital media and software design since there is an aboriginal community with a strong artistic sense. Therefore, Elliot Lake College needs a website to promote it and let people who are browsing the website know more information about the college. They also wish more people who can be attracted by the website and apply for curriculums in the college.

Target Market

There are two personas, and both are grade 12 students. High school students are the website’s primary target market because they could be the future students of Elliot Lake College. Also, they are interested in the design fields, such as digital design and software design, so when they are searching for design programs on the Internet, they might browse the website of Elliot Lake College. Therefore, the website must be easy to search or browse for future students to find the program they are interested in. Also, the website should be attractive for future students in order to let them know the advances of the college.

Since Elliot Lake area relates with the Serpent River First Nation and Huron Robinson Treaty land, Elliot Lake area has a strong artistic sense.
