My Doctor App

UX Group Project / User Research & UI Design

A medication application that helps people to get medical resources online, such as book appointments from online doctors, chat with AI,arrange appointments and medical records, and call emergency.

Project Overview

Challenge

During the Covid-19 virus, a lot of people worry about that if they go to hospitals, they might affect by the virus. Also, it's difficult to schedule an appointment with the doctor during this time. Therefor, create a medication application to help people find a doctor online, schedule an appointment with specialist, get a prescription or referral from doctors, connect with pharmacies, user profile, and emergency call.

User Research & User Stories

Scenarios

John starts his morning not feeling great. He has been feeling tired for the past few days, keeps coughing and cannot sleep during the night. The symptoms are getting worse, and he is worried he might have contracted covid-19 as he lives in NYC which is an area of risk and concern regarding this disease. John has a history of heart disease causing high blood pressure which is getting worse because of stress. Because he is worried about making his existing condition worse, and he does not want to risk others if he is a Covid-19 carrier, he decided to use an online doctor app.

User Needs

  • John needs to be tested for infectious disease.
  • John needs a refill of his blood pressure medication.
  • John cannot be exposed to others (such as in waiting rooms).

User Wants

  • John wants to book an appointment with his favourite doctor with a flexible schedule.
  • John wants a safe option with a low risk of spreading disease.
  • John wants to feel less stress.
  • John would want a service that is accessible.

User Problems

  • John might have Covid-19 and might need accommodation to prevent getting other people infected.
  • John might be bad with technology.
  • John might want to talk to a person without being in contact with them.
  • John might not be infected and does not want to get infected and wants to avoid busy areas.
  • John needs his medication, which is running low and is needed.
  • John is experiencing stress which is making his conditions worse.

Design Solutions

Design Solutions

  • The app “My Doctor” is designed for the mobile platform because most people have their cell phones and internet, so people can easily use the “My Doctor” app on their cell phones no matter where they are.
  • The colour theme of the “My Doctor” app is using monochrome colour to maintain consistency. Also, the lake green colour that used on the app can make people feel calm and peaceful. The pink colour used on the emergency button can give people a warning and a clear label.
  • There are mainly 6 features on the “My Doctor” app, i.e., “Find Doctor and Book Appointment”, “Chat with AI”, “My Appointments”, “Medical Records”, “Emergency”, and “My Profile”.
