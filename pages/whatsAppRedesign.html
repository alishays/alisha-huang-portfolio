WhatsApp Redesign

UX Group Project / User Research & UI Design

A concept for redesigning WhatsApp. Especially redesign the function of Contact to make users can easily manage.

Project Overview

Project Brief

WhatsApp gives features for free texting, voice call and video call. Since it is a widely popular and used application, there are both some eye-catching features and some frustrations. The frustration which user face while using WhatsApp is the auto-download of the media, privacy while connecting with someone, a limited number of media sharing at a time, and group chat adding feature...etc. As a result, in our design, we use the idea that some are included in WhatsApp like chat, calls, story, account settings, camera and group chat to minimize the frustration and problems we collect from the survey, new features and solutions will be needed.

Design Process

Market Research & User Research

Market Research

In these recent years, mobile messaging apps are some of the most common and easy platforms to enable instant messaging. The population of messaging apps have become much bigger than social media, with billions of users. Spjic (2019) states that there are 3 billion people using mobile messaging applications in 2020. Within this huge market, WhatsApp is the top messaging app that people use. There are more than 2 billion users use WhatsApp, and number of users using WhatsApp are growing very fast every year. The top reason billions of people use WhatsApp is because of its worldwide pool of users, and the WhatsApp can be installed on any device, such as Apple, Android, or Windows devices. The second largest messaging app platform is Messenger, this app is used by only two-third of the people compared to the number of people who use WhatsApp

Persona

Problem Analysis

Scenarios

WhatsApp gives features for free texting, voice call and video call. Since it is a widely popular and used application, there are both some eye-catching features and some frustrations. The frustration which user face while using WhatsApp is the auto-download of the media, privacy while connecting with someone, a limited number of media sharing at a time, and group chat adding feature...etc. As a result, in our design, we use the idea that some are included in WhatsApp like chat, calls, story, account settings, camera and group chat to minimize the frustration and problems we collect from the survey, new features and solutions will be needed.

User Needs

  • Privacy while adding friend or giving number to someone.
  • Account setting where user can set their own preference for downloading the media.
  • There should be more instructions and direction or those who are new on the app so that they can accessible the application easily.

User Wants

  • A QR code to scan so that adding friend would be much easier.
  • A way that adding friend get much easier and secured.
  • Easily to find the contact.
  • To know more about what friends did recently.

User Frustrations

  • Need to share contact number to the person we don’t know but want to add to friend list.
  • It automatically downloads the media and lowers the storage capacity.
  • Cannot add a person in a group if you are not an admin of the group.

Design Solutions

Design Solutions

  • WhatsApp is a messaging app designed for both mobile and web platforms. We decided to focus on the mobile platform because it is much more common, and features could be focused more easily onto it.
  • We designed our survey questions together, and a total of 20 people responded to them. Our survey questions were different from the interview questions. A total of 4 people were interviewed. After analyzing the data from the survey and interviews, we discussed the major features and flaws of WhatsApp. The team all sketched their own versions of the app, and when they were all finished, we discussed what to keep from each sketch for improvement. Once everything was complete, we reviewed everything together to finalize and polish our ideas.
  • We use a monochrome colour scheme to maintain consistency.
  • There are mainly 6 features on the WhatsApp app, which include: login and sign-up page, chat page, creating group chat, contact page, add new friend page, story page, settings page.
